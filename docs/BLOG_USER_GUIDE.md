# Blog Feature - Quick Start Guide

## For Approved Members

### How to Submit a Blog Post

1. **Navigate to the Blog Page**
   - Click on "Blog" in the navigation menu
   - Or go to `/blog`

2. **Click "Submit Your Story"**
   - This button appears only for approved members
   - Opens a submission form dialog

3. **Fill Out the Form**
   - **Category** (required): Choose from:
     - Campus Memories: Share memories from your DU days
     - Published Articles: Share articles, research, or professional insights
     - Talent Hub: Showcase creative work, achievements, or projects
   - **Title** (required): Enter a compelling title (max 200 characters)
   - **Excerpt** (required): Write a brief summary (max 300 characters, shown in blog listing)
   - **Content** (required): Write your full story or article
   - **Featured Image URL** (optional): Add an image URL to represent your post

4. **Submit for Review**
   - Click "Submit for Review"
   - Your post will be sent to admins for approval
   - You'll see a success message confirming submission

5. **Wait for Admin Approval**
   - Admins will review your submission
   - If approved, your post will be published on the blog
   - If rejected, you'll see the rejection reason (check your profile/submissions page)

## For Admins

### How to Review Blog Submissions

1. **Go to Admin Dashboard**
   - Navigate to `/admin`
   - Click on the "Blog Posts" tab

2. **Review Pending Posts**
   - See all pending submissions in the "Pending" tab
   - View post details: title, excerpt, author, date, category

3. **Preview Full Content**
   - Click the "View" (eye) icon to preview the full post
   - Review title, excerpt, content, and featured image

4. **Approve or Reject**
   - **To Approve**:
     - Click the "Approve & Publish" button
     - Post immediately becomes visible on the blog
   - **To Reject**:
     - Click the "Reject" button
     - Enter a rejection reason (visible to the author)
     - Click "Reject Post" to confirm

5. **Manage Approved/Rejected Posts**
   - View approved posts in the "Approved" tab
   - View rejected posts in the "Rejected" tab
   - Delete any post using the trash icon

## Tips for Great Blog Posts

### Writing Tips
- **Catchy Title**: Make it attention-grabbing and descriptive
- **Strong Excerpt**: Summarize the main idea in 1-2 sentences
- **Clear Content**: Use paragraphs, be concise, tell a story
- **Personal Touch**: Share your unique perspective and experiences
- **Proofread**: Check for spelling and grammar errors

### Image Guidelines
- Use high-quality images (min 1200px wide recommended)
- Ensure you have rights to use the image
- Relevant to your post content
- Use image hosting services like Imgur, Cloudinary, or upload to your own server
- Provide a direct image URL (ending in .jpg, .png, etc.)

### Category Selection
- Choose the category that best fits your content
- Campus Memories: Personal, nostalgic, experience-focused
- Published Articles: Formal, informative, research-based
- Talent Hub: Creative, showcase-oriented, achievement-focused

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Approval time depends on admin availability. Usually within 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I edit my post after submission?**
A: Currently, editing is not available. You'll need to submit a new post. (Feature coming soon!)

**Q: What happens if my post is rejected?**
A: You'll see the rejection reason and can submit a revised version as a new post.

**Q: Can I submit multiple posts?**
A: Yes! There's no limit on submissions.

**Q: Who can see my post before approval?**
A: Only you and admins can see pending posts. Published posts are visible to everyone.

**Q: Can I delete my post after it's published?**
A: Contact an admin to request post removal.
